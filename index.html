ABOUT US

In 1962 a small group of Kansas City area Model A ford enthusiasts gathered together to form a local Club. The name they adopted was "Heart of America Model A Ford Club". The club is now organized as a non-profit corporation under the laws of the State of Missouri. The Club is governed by a board operating under a system of by-laws developed by the membership and updated on a periodic basis.

Author's Message

Membership in the Heart of America Model A Ford Club is open to everyone, and ownership of a Model A is not a requirement. Your membership will put you in contact with very knowledgeable Ford Model A owners who are always happy to help a fellow enthusiast. Please feel free to drop by our meetings or events.
